Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this web site is to give ratings and reviews for some of my favorite games based on the time I have spent playing them, what they have to offer, and the type of game they are. These ratings and reviews are made to help others find new games to try.

Audience

This web site is for those who want to try out new games but are unsure what game to try or for those who want to learn more about certain games.
